Timing (April 2026 note). (t=0a) Issuer announces σ; (t=0b) investors form xe, deposit iff E[Ui]≥vi; M locks in and is fixed at (t=0c): M=(E[Ui]−rb)/(rg−rb). The issuer then chooses x to maximise M·E[π] with M constant, so ∂M/∂x does not enter the x-FOC. σ̄=(1−β−η)r/[β(R−r)], T*=(1−β−η)/β. Regime I (σ<σ̄): xI*=x̃=r(R−1)/(R−r). Regime II (σ≥σ̄): E[πII]=(1−σ)x(R−1) is linear in x ⇒ xII*=1 (corner, not joint-commitment interior). (BW) Solution B dominates iff (R−1)−T*/2 > r(R−1)²/[2(R−r)] (net carry at x=1 vs fire-sale carry at x̃). [G] ≡ I: government replication matches Regime I; xG*=x̃, σG*=σ̂I. At interior optima, sign(WP−WG)=sign(wII−wG)=sign(BW); WG>WP possible only under B-constrained (σP=σ̄, xP=1).
