Optimal Stablecoin Contracts under Limited Commitment

Interactive simulation — Wesley Tan, Wesleyan University 2026 · Advisor: Prof. Ryuichiro Izumi

Model summary. Three parties: issuer, impatient investors (fraction λ~U[0,1]), patient investors. Contract: carry rate σ and illiquid share x. Regime I (σ < σ̄): fire sales when congested. Regime II (σ ≥ σ̄): patient stayers block fire sales by paying transfer T* per deferred unit. Government [G] replicates Regime I. Aggregate welfare W = M·E[Ω] uses market size M, not per-depositor surplus.
